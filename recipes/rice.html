Rice Recipe

Un arroz esponjoso y perfecto como acompañamiento, sencillo y rápido de preparar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice, butter, and salt to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
