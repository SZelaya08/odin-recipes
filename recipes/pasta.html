Pasta Recipe

Home

Una pasta sencilla y sabrosa, con una salsa ligera de tomate y ajo que se prepara en minutos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in crushed tomatoes and dried basil.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Drain cooked pasta and toss with the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
