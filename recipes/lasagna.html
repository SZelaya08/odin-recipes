Lasagna Recipe

Una lasaña casera, rica en capas de queso, carne y salsa, ideal para compartir en familia.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, mix ricotta cheese with basil.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
